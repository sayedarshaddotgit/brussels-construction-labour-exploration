# Methodology

## Research Objective

To explore the role of migrant labour in Brussels' construction sector using publicly available Belgian Census 2011 data.

## Data Discovery

The project began with exploration of the Belgian open-data catalogue (DCAT) and multiple Census 2011 hypercube datasets.

After evaluating several datasets, HC22 was selected because it contains information on:

- Citizenship
- Economic sector
- Place of work
- Age
- Sex

## Analysis

The analysis focused on:

- Construction sector workers (NACE Section F)
- Citizenship composition
- Regional comparison
- Age structure
